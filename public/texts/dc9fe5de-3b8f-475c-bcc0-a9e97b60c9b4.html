ABOUT CLUB TRANSMISSION

CLUB TRANSMISSION is your queer as fuck night in Amsterdam.
For DIY DJ’s who want to get everyone dancing to their favorite tunes and those who wanna show off their moves on the dance floor.

We aim to create a safe club for BIPOC and Queer people, a space to meet new cuties and a space that can transfer itself from one location to another.
