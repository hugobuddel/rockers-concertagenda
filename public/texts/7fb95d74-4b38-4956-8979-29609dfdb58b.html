EDITRIX (Massachusetts, USA)

Editrix consists of Wendy Eisenberg on guitar and vocals, Steve Cameron on bass and Josh Daniel on drums.

The band’s music can be described as a fusion of punk, jazz, and experimental sounds. Their debut album, “Tell Me I’m Bad,” showcases their eclectic style and has received widespread praise.

Their latest album, “Editrix II: Editrix Goes to Hell,” has also received praise from music critics. According to Pitchfork‘s review, “Like Fugazi before them, Editrix takes punk’s framework and stretches it to its breaking point, pushing listeners to question orthodoxy in all its forms—ethical, ontological, and musical.”

Editrix is known for their high-energy live performances, where they push the boundaries of genres and challenge their audiences. They have played at various music festivals and venues across the United States, sharing the stage with bands like Deerhoof.

Het knotsgekke duo Behringer Escape Plan ontmoette elkaar in Amsterdam met een gedeelde liefde voor elektronica, complexe ritmes en synthesizers. In de loop der jaren ontwikkelden ze hun eigen elektronische tools die hun instrumenten uitbreidden tot een symbiose tussen drums en synthesizers. Men regelt de toonhoogte en het geluid van zijn synths, terwijl verschillende elementen van de drums de noten van de gespeelde toonhoogtes triggeren. Dit creëert een omgeving waarin timing, harmonie en geluid vrijelijk kunnen worden verkend met minder beperkingen in tempo, arrangementen en songwriting. Verwacht wankele grooves, gedurfde basgeluiden en snelle melodieën.
