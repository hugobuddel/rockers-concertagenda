After months of pressing delays, the arrival of the vinyls for ADVERSARY’s “NONE WILL MOURN” EP is finally in sight.

To celebrate the physical release of their 8-song debut on Blindsided Records, ADVERSARY proudly welcomes you to the official EP release show on Saturday August 13th that will also mark the band’s first appearance in the Netherlands.

Mark your calendars for an evening of surgically precise Hardcore / Deathmetal violence with special guests in Mouflon (NL), Voidcrawler (NL) and Swell (NL) set to join the occasion.

*Dagger Threat is replaced by Mouflon *
