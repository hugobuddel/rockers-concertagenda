A Night of Harsh Noise Worship

The Rita (CA) is the noise project of Vancouver sound artist Sam McKinlay. A pioneer of harsh noise wall (HNW) who began in the late 1990s with Italian horror-influenced releases and has since continually pushed onward, unwilling to plough the same terrain for too long.

Black Leather Jesus (US)
Cult harsh noise group since 1989, founded by Richard Ramirez in Houston, Texas utilizing sheet metal, found objects and minimal electronic equipment to create dense walls of noise and feedback.

Vomir (FR)
“no ideas, no change, no development, no entertainment, no remorse”. Pioneering harsh noise wall project by Romain Perrot.

JSH (SE)
No frills harsh noise from Stockholm by Johan Strömvall Hammarstedt.

Misère (US/NL)
Primitive harsh noise duo comprising of Sisto Rossi (Wallkeeper, Foxhole, etc) and Jim Breedveld (Geseling).
