BURLERS

BURLERS is a Dutch four piece from Amsterdam/Horst. Their music merges traditional hardcore punk and garage punk, resulting in a fiery combination of razor-sharp riffs, thundering basslines, and frenzied energy. Plain and simple Burlers = a punk band.
