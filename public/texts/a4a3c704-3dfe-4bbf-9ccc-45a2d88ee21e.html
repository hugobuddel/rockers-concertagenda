PLOT, An Evening of Sound & Image, with Oorbeek and a host of other acts.

PLOT is a one-evening mini festival with various artists that perform with, before, after or around OORBEEK, using sound, image, light, shadows, props & fire.

OORBEEK w/ WINEKE GARTZ + PETER BOGERS + DJ SMACKER (aka Mister Blowpipe, Moskwood, Minnypops) + DOGTIMESLIDESCRATCHINGSOCIETY + MAARTEN VINKENOOG + HARALD AUSTBø (TBC) & MORE!!!

Being received as ‘one of the highlights of Haarlems Boring Festival’ in January where they ‘propagated ultimate musical freedom’ (Peter Bruyn in Noord Hollands Dagblad)
Oorbeek will finally play in Amsterdam again.
Amsterdams art band Oorbeek (formed in 2001) will start free sound amidst a live audience. This evening is also the long awaited presentation of their LP “KAVEL”.
