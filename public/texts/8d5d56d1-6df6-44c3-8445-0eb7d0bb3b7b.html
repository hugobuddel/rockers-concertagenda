Richard Walker
Richard is a comedian, raconteur and unemployment benefits expert. He runs his own monthly show (Oosterpoke Comedy) in Amsterdam, performs to sold out venues around Europe, and is taking a half show to this year's Edinburgh fringe.

Sharon Em
Sharon Em fluctuates between trying hard and hardly trying. Mostly found on her couch plotting how to realise her life goal of having pints with Tommy Tiernan (main life inspiration). Or pondering if straight bananas will ever be a thing! Her best friends describe her as 'who?'

Aleksandr Pereverzev
Aleksandr Pereverzev relates well to international audiences, having performed stand-up comedy in Switzerland, Georgia and now the Netherlands! Gathering inspiration from his life experiences, his comedy is engaging, witty and dark. All delivered with a wry smile.

Lara Mikocki
Lara has the charm of wind from a toddler’s behind, and a burp from its front. Living in Amsterdam, but hailing from Australia with Austrian parents, she is a bonafide human mongrel that has spelling and identity issues. It’s ok, she doesn’t bite, she only nibbles at your funny bone. She’ll tackle mental health, have a live panic attack and talk about anything to do with the butt - she’s a derrière connoisseur. That was hard to spell. See you soon!

Arsel Hasan
From India to the Netherlands, Arsel chased the perfect combo of weed and beef. Now stuck here as an adult, he hilariously shares his misadventures on stage. Get ready for a wild night of Amsterdam tales that'll leave you in stitches!

Odette van der Molen
Winner of the Comedy Talent Award 2019, Odette van der Molen is made for the spotlights! With her self-deprecating, sharp humour and spot-on characterisations, she challenges and ridicules modern life and her generation gap, with her relatable topics. 

With her delightful Dutch accent, Odette is likely to kill the Queen’s English on the spot, but you won’t mind, as she’ll make up for it! Odette joined the English-speaking comedy scene in recent years and already is making a name for herself, leaving expats and tourists in stitches.She plays frequently in the comedyshows at Boomchicago and Comedycafé Amsterdam.

She can be sharp, honest and direct, not sparing herself one single bit, and not entirely free of any self-embarrassment. Odette effortlessly connects with her audience in both a charming and playful way!
