**As Friends Rust **
Celebrating their new album "any joy"
Consisting of former members of Culture, Morning Again, Roosevelt, and Shai Hulud, the Gainesville, FL, troupe of As Friends Rust integrates their hybrid punk and emo influences to result in a styling of melodic hardcore. Although critically compared to the likes of Turning Point, Dag Nasty, and Avail, their lyrical dealings of personal relationships and how people interact with each other makes As Friends Rust stand out among their peers.

Goodlife Records released the band's debut EP, The Fists of Time, in 1998, followed by the their self-titled full-length on Doghouse Records in 1999. A split with Discount was issued two years later, followed only months later by the full-length Won. The EP A Young Trophy Band in the Parlance of Our Times appeared in 2002 through Equal Vision.

In 2020, As Friends Rust released the EP, Up From the Muck, through Unity Worldwide Records and Stick to the Core Records, containing its first-recorded material in eighteen years. On June 22, 2023, the band announced that its sophomore full-length album, Any Joy, will be released on August 18, 2023, via End Hits Records.
