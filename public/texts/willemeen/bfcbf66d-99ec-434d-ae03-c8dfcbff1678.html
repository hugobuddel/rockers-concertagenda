Methedrine
The first idea of the Methedrine project takes form after ‘80s crossover/d-beat pioneers Upset Noise disbands after a brief reunion in 2017. Drummer Bone, singer Lou and recently acquired guitarist Mark start looking for new elements for a fresh start and a new band. They find them in Dario (guitar) and Bob (bass): the watchword are Motorhead, first thrash and HC attitude.
It’s Methedrine 2.0, that keeps up with the d-beat/crossover legacy, yet expanding their sonic landscape with darker and broader influences. A new full length Lp is out now!
