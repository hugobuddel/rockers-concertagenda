Releaseparty of the debut LP of mathcore/noise-punk adventurers ‘a fungus’, one of the best kept secrets of the Amsterdam underground. Their debut LP ‘It already does that’ is out 20/05 through Subroutine. Their sound is characterized by fusion of indie rock, emo, post-hardcore and post-rock.
For fans of Slint, Black Midi, Karate, The Van Pelt and Blonde Redhead.

Last year, the band did a streaming show from OCCII. Thursday June 16 they return for their releaseparty, with local friends, bands and DJs.
