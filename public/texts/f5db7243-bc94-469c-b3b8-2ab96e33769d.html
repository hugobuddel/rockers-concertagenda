Action Beat started in 2004 in Bletchley, UK. So far they have played over 700 shows in Europe and North America. The live band normally plays with 3 guitars, baritone, bass, and 2-3 drummers. We toured the East and Mid West States for 23 consecutive dates in 2016, and completed the USA by touring the West Coast in 2019.

Oma is an electronic splinter group comprising members of UK-based noise-punk DIY underground stalwarts Action Beat and G.W. Sok, united by a shared desire to explore electronic music elements. Samples, electronics, spoken words, and glimmers of hope.
