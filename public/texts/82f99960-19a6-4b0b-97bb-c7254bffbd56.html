2022 Jack’s World Series of Darts Finals tickets on General Sale

The world’s top darts stars will return to AFAS Live in September for the Jack’s World Series of Darts Finals, with tickets now on general sale.
The televised premier event will see 24 players in action from September 16-18, with Michael van Gerwen, Peter Wright, Gerwyn Price and reigning champion Jonny Clayton all set to compete in the Dutch capital.

The tournament will also feature qualifiers from the global World Series events being held this year in America, Denmark, Australia and New Zealand, with history-maker Fallon Sherrock among the competitors.
Tickets went on General Sale at 1200 CET on Wednesday March 23 – get yours now to secure a seat at this popular event!
