One Another

The greatest UK/Russian collaboration since the Second World War, One
Another are a Utrecht-based post-punk/darkwave duo comprised of Sam Rink
and Laurie Rae. Formed in 2022 and inspired by the likes of Drab
Majesty, Aphex Twin, Cocteau Twins and John Maus, they present a
genre-bending musical experience. This is their first ever live concert
to support the release of their upcoming EP “The Chasm”.

 

 

Kboy en de goedzo jongens

You may or may not know kboy en de goedzo jongens from their debut EP
MUKUS MENSENSLIJM. Or maybe you know them from that time they bummed a
ciggie off you. This childish post-punk band (which means shouting about
boobs etc.) is a big fan of artists like The Garden, The B-52’s, Kleenex
and Cake. Are you a fan of those bands as well and do you have somewhat
low standards? Then come and have a look!!

 

Quincy & The David

Quincy & The David are a DIY band from Zwolle. For fans of (post) punk,
songs that don’t exceed 3 minutes, mosh pits and screaming, combined
with a sound inspired by The Cure, Goat Girl and Fontaines DC. The final
ingredient is lyrics like finding out all your heroes from back in the
day are sexist and whether or not they dare to wear makeup. You can hear
all these ingredients on their debut album Purple Moon, Yellow Sun.
