ACTION BEAT w/ G.W SOK

Started in 2004 as a duo playing at house-parties. Eventually growing into a monster of around 3 guitars, a bass and between 1-4 drummers; Action Beat, ‘The Noise band from Bletchley’, toured extensively throughout Europe between 2007-2015 playing semi-improvised, energetic noise rock.
While on the road, they recorded eight LPs. “The World Is Fucked But I Feel Fine” was released in May 2017 and is their second full-length with former The Ex singer, GW Sok, collaborating on vocals.
