The legendary rock band Mother’s Finest is going on tour again! The American group has created a sound described as a fusion of funk, rock, gospel, R&B, metal, and jazz, taken directly from the streets to the stage and the studio. The versatility and originality of Mother’s Finest broke many barriers, making them one of the most legendary bands ever to represent this genre, and they have influenced countless other bands.

Mother’s Finest consists of Joyce Kennedy, Glenn Murdock on lead vocals, Juan van Dunk on bass, Gary “Mo” Moore on lead guitar, John Hayes on lead guitar, and drummer Dion Murdock. Together, they are incomparable in their ability to play a show you’ll remember. After more than 50 years on tour, they are still one of the best live bands on Earth.
