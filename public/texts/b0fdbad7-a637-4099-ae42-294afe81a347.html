Conference Women in Dutch punk, OCCII Amsterdam, 18 June 2022
Last December, because of the coronavirus pandemic, at the last minute we had to cancel the conference of the Punk Scholars Network the Netherlands at OCCII, Amstelveenseweg 134 in Amsterdam with as its main theme Women in punk, replacing it with a short webinar on Punk and venues.
We have now decided there will be a face to face conference on Women in punk in the Netherlands in OCCII on Saturday 18 June. We try to make it accessible on the internet as well.
And, in early December 2022, as part of the worldwide face to face PSN conference, a conference of more than one day, at the VU University in Amsterdam, a full day with as its theme Women in punk, international.
On 18 June in OCCII,  lectures.  British scholar/first wave punk musician Helen Reddington will interview four Dutch female musicians, representing four waves in Dutch punk, from 1977 to the 2020s.
There will be film,  by Helen Reddington. And a new film on Dutch first wave punk.
There will be food and bands playing at OCCII.
Spread the news everyone, save the date 18 June!
