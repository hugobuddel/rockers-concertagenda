Nanowar Of Steel

“We take parody seriously”, aldus Nanowar Of Steel. Sinds 2003 steekt dit gezelschap de draak met rock en metal in compleet geflipte outfits en dito songtitels. Neem ‘Armpits Of Immortals’ of ‘Hail To Liechtenstein’ en je ligt al krom. Daarnaast maken ze ook nog eens folkmetal met Italiaanse teksten, wat nog meer gekkigheid teweegbrengt waar je allesbehalve gallisch van wordt. Rare jongens, die Romeinen. Het levert de band overigens serieus succes op, zoals een deal met Napalm Records en festivalweides die stuk worden gefeest. Valhalleluja!

Frozen Crown

Frozen Crown is a female fronted Power Metal band infused with Classic Heavy Metal and Symphonic metal vibes.
Born in 2018, the band released three albums, “The Fallen King”, “Crowned In Frost” and “Winterbane” which gave the band huge media exposure – over 30 million views total on YouTube for their singles – and made it possible to be featured as special guest on the 2020 Dragonforce European tour, amongst many other accomplishments. Starting with the third album “Winterbane” sees Frozen Crown amplifyied the classic heavy and power metal components of their sound and toned down the symphonic influences even more, resulting in an aggressive and monolithic sound thanks to the tasteful riffs and guitar leads of mastermind and songwriter Federico Mondelli and the astonishing female vocals provided by talented singer Giada “Jade” Etro, broadening Frozen Crown’s musical landscape while keeping intact an uncommon taste for haunting melodies and catchy refrains.
During its short lifespan, Frozen Crown played two European tours, one Japanese headlining tour and several shows throughout Europe including Sabaton Open Air and Metal Fest Plzen, sharing the stages with bands like Sabaton, Amon Amarth, Arch Enemy, Dragonforce, Stratovarius and more.
