Audio/visual band playing deathrock, synth punk, post-punk, ambient, and ethereal soundscapes..
Comprised of Noel Skum (Andrew Clinco of Drab Majesty), Z. Oro (Aaron Montaigne of Antioch Arrow/Heroin/DBC) on vocals and drums, and Mico Frost (Brian Tarney of Guardian) on synths and electric bass.
Support by Utrecht synth punkers DAISY DAISY.
