On Tuesday 30 January 2024, Slaughter to Prevail is coming to Zappa, Antwerp for a night of unrelenting brutality and bone-shattering metal madness. Get ready to be engulfed by the sheer intensity of Alex Terrible’s guttural vocals, accompanied by the relentless force of Slaughter to Prevail’s crushing riffs and thunderous beats. Originally formed in Russia, the band made the bold decision to relocate to Florida, USA, in the wake of the Russia-Ukraine war. Firmly standing against violence and conflict, they carry a strong message of unity and peace through their music.
The band’s debut album “Misery Sermon” (2017) solidified their place in the extreme metal scene, showcasing a blend of technical prowess and unrelenting aggression that has garnered a dedicated fanbase worldwide.Brace yourself for the reign of Slaughter to Prevail, the sonic architects of chaos!
