Roadburn, ons internationaal vermaarde festival voor grensverleggende heavy music, strijkt in 2022 eindelijk weer neer in Tilburg voor een fysieke editie. Milena Eva & Thomas Sciarone van GGGOLDDD zijn de curators. Full of Hell en SLIFT zijn de artists-in-residence. Op de vrijdag staan de volgende acts op het programma:

Albatre | Alcest (performing 'Écailles de Lune') | Alice Hubble (performing 'Hexentanzplatz') | Amnesia Scanner | Atonia (a collaboration between Wyatt E., Five Hierophant & MC Slice) | Cloud Rat ('Do Not Let Me Off This Cliff' electronic set) | Faceless Entity | FREJA | Full of Hell (artist-in-residence) | GGGOLDDD (performing 'This Shame Should Not Be Mine') | Gott | Green Lung (performing 'Black Harvest') | HEALTH | James Kent & Johannes Persson (performing commissioned piece 'Final Light') | Karin Park | Koldovstvo (performing 'Ни царя, ни бога') | Kollaps | Motor!k | PoiL Ueda | Primitive Man | Radar Men From The Moon (Acid Metal' electronic set) | Red Kite | Severant | SLIFT (artist-in-residence) | Sum Of R (performing 'Lahbryce') | Supersonic Blues | Svalbard | TAU & The Drones of Praise | Warhorse | Wiegedood (performing 'There Is Always Blood At The End Of The Road') | YODOK III (Dirk Serries artist-in-residence) | Zetra
