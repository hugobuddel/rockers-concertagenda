Deze tour wordt verplaatst naar woensdag 28 juni 2023. Gekochte tickets blijven geldig. Ticketkopers ontvangen een mail met meer info. 

Statement van de band: 
"It is with extreme disappointment that we are having to make some amends on our upcoming European tour. The fucked Aussie dollar along with spiralling touring costs mean we are forced to shorten the period. Most of the tour remains unaffected but we will no longer be able to visit England, Scotland or Holland on this trip. Please note, we are NOT CANCELLING THESE SHOWS but rescheduling them to the Summer of 2023."
