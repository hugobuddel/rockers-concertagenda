Deze show is afgelast. Er komt geen vervangende datum. Ticketkopers krijgen een mail met meer informatie omtrent restitutie van tickets. Statement van de band: 

"Due to circumstances beyond our control it is unfortunately not the right time for the Night Flight Orchestra to embark on a club tour in Europe this May/June. We are of course very disappointed and are anxiously waiting to take to the skies. On a positive note, all the planned festivals this summer are still on and we cannot wait to see you there! We also have some other exciting news up our sleeves. Stay tuned! Up, up and away!

With love, The Night Flight Orchestra"
