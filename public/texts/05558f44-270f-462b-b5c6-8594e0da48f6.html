Youff (Belgium)

Picture by Jostijn Ligtvoet – Instagram

Youff is a Belgian noiserock band with members of 30.000 Monkies, Crowd Of Chairs, Mesher, Joeri Chipsfingers, Maze, and some others.
Known for their energetic live acts and intense sound, Youff tries to combine a dissonant harmony with a raw punk attitude.

FFO: Arab on Radar, Glenn Branca, Urbanus, Fritkot

Designer Violence

Wild synthesizers, untamed beats and emotional vocals. Two women screaming in
your face. Designer Violence formed out of a shared passion for underground 80s and 90s
artists. Armed with an arsenal of synths, samplers and a bass VI, the band’s sound crosses
post-punk, wave, techno and industrial with raw and wayward flair.

FFO: Nine Inch Nails, HEALTH, Lady Gaga, HIDE
