WAP SHOO WAP presents THE COVIDS SINGLE RELEASE PARTY with special guests NEED!

THE COVIDS (7″ single release show)

Exactly one year ago Amsterdam’s The Covids played their very first live show right here at OCCII. And now they’re back presenting their first vinyl 7″ single released on Wap Shoo Wap Records! Their  sound is raw, catchy and energetic… mixing the best of ’77 punkrock with some later 80’s new wave influences.

Watch: https://youtu.be/hNaN0I2P3HQ
Listen: https://spoti.fi/3uL2g3g

NEED (special reunion show)

After calling it quits in 2019, Amsterdam punkrock enfant terribles NEED are back from the grave with a vengeance! The band features members from Aux Raus and Local Spastics and is inspired by the sounds of The Wipers and The Stooges. Mayhem guaranteed…

Watch: https://youtu.be/5oFlgQFwx2k
Listen: https://spoti.fi/3uLAFPH

After the bands the DJ’s from the Wap Shoo Wap Records wrecking crew will spin the best rock ‘n roll, punk, garage, soul and rhythm ‘n blues records for your dancing pleasure!

Starting out in 2016 as a booking agency and monthly party night in Amsterdam, Wap Shoo Wap Records is now also a record label which specializes in releasing punkrock and rock ‘n roll vinyl from new local and international artists + reissues of hard to find gems from the past.

Check out more about our releases here: https://www.wapshoowaprecords.com/
