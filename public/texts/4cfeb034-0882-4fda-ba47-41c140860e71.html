RIMOJEKI – Space Duo, Berlin, Germany.
RimoJeki is an awesomely entertaining and innovative psychedelic experience that incorporates visionary music, ground-breaking video art and out-of-this-world outfits.
Their music is a brilliant mix of heavy danceable beats, piercing catchy bass lines, stoner guitar riffs, wave synth noise, spaced-out Theremin, and vocals that swing between post-punk and heavenly ethereal melodies.
Rimojeki’s performance is a wild live tapestry of aliens, colors, psychedelia, mythologies, science, future, love and darkness, with an underline of wicked humor.

DE KRATERS
De Kraters is een One Man Synthband toegeweid aan de gouden tijd van de analoge elektro. Op een set klassieke machines verweven ze explosieve synthpunk met elementaire Italo. Stemmen zijn getransformeerd in vocoder – megafoon – robots. De Kraters spelen alles live, gedreven door de vereende kracht van hun helden, Giorgio Moroder, Andi Arroganti, Bobby Orlando; van Grauzone’s Eisbaer tot Medio Mutante’s Inestable.
