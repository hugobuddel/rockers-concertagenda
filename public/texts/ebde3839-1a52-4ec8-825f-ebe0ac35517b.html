Dreaming Demons

Leffe Goldstein presents an alternative future perspective. HIs drawings are a black and white vision of an absurd world where people no longer house. A world where only their demons survived. He is intrigued by places once full of life and loved by its inhabitants and its builders. The drawings consist of real existing places or objects. It could be buildings or pieces of machinery, planes, helicopters and cars left alone to decay. Objects that were invented and build by real hands by real people and were full of life. These people are no longer there and might be long forgotten. But take a good look and you can see their demons are still in there, breeding an alternative future.
