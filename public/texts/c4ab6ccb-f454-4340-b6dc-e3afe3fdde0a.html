SERVE

This evening, SERVE will be debuting their full-length ‘The Light Outlives The Sun’.
SERVE’ tells a story about the effect mental illness can have on a person. It explores in graphic detail the vicious cycle kickstarted by depression and perpetuated by substance abuse and feelings of isolation & guilt. It does this set to the vicious drums, heavily distorted riffs and harrowing vocals the band bring forth. All in all ‘SERVE’ is not for the faint of heart.

 

Torn From Oblivion

Torn From Oblivion is a Dutch deathcore band that originated from Eindhoven. They bring the
aggression of deathcore, experiment with progressive riffs and mix in hardcore to fulfill their vision of
modern aggressive music. The group consists of 5 young musicians: vocalist Mees Stevens, guitarists
Bob van Kempen and Joep Morsink, bass player Kane van Diepen and drummer Luc van Dijk. The live
shows are energetic and this is where the band truly shines, it’s something you don’t want to miss.
Torn From Oblivion are committed to leave their mark on the music scene, and so they will.

 

TIGERKNIFE 

TIGERKNIFE is a hardcore-/metalband from Rotterdam. Groovy riffs and slamming breakdowns
combined with relatable, hardhitting lyrics that everyone can sing along to. TIGERKNIFE will
blow you away with an energized show!
