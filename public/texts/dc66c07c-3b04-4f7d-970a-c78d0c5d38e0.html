THIRD EGO
Third Ego is opening up. In early 2020 four seasoned punkrock veterans (ex-members of NRA, Brat Pack, NEED, Sellfish and many more), got together in the depths of Amsterdam’s Vondelbunker. While the world outside got layered under a gloomy blanket, they crafted their sound accordingly. Bringing influences from the roaring 80’s to the boisterous ‘90, Third Ego encapsulates decades of rock and punk.
