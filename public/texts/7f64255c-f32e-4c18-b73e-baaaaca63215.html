Spitskop is a celebration of breaking predictability, routine and rules. It is about being analog in digital days. Spitskop is an evening, a night, a bar, live music, record spinners, and you (dancing). A party anticipating friction in honor of counter culture. Let’s rejoice and experience music once more.

Line up:
Pol
are two siblings inspired by new wave and the 80s in general with artists such as Visage, The Cure, Japan, New Order, Depeche Mode and Duran Duran. In their work they try to replace digitally produced sounds for analog synths and drum machine samples. Some of their work is mixed by Dave Bascombe (Depeche Mode, Tears For Fears, Erasure).


httpv://www.youtube.com/watch?v=channel/UCtYkpUXNOHG73jv9wl9KX3Q

Frankie Teardrop
is a synth-punk rock trio who decided that the best way to channel incredulity and frustration is through music; loud, uncompromised and catchy. The message they spread? A humorous, cynical point of view on the mad, mad modern world. On the 6th of July 2022 they released their first full Length album “Punk Deluxe”
https://linktr.ee/20yearoldfrankie

Anan Striker / Strictly Tapes (oldschool hiphop, hiphop electro)
is playing strictly vinyl & tapes as a way to criticize the digital age. Go back in time with the dusty left-field sound of Striker’s compact cassettes and finger-scratch-techniques. Revolutionary, raw and healthy.
https://echobox.radio/shows/strictly-tapes/

KOSMOS (new wave; south america; italo disco)
is setting things in motion by connecting the dots between different parts of this planet – switching up electronics from (not so) past eras. Attentie! Attenzione! Atenção! Genre-defying!

Grim b2b Nina (electro)
is collecting obscurities from the deepest caverns of the internet, archives full of ODM and rare digs: they collect it all for you and the world. Get ready for a tour de horizon of atmospheric electro, ambient and ingenious club sounds.
