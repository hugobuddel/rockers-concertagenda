Pretty//Ugly presents:

Eli Winter
Eli Winter is a musician and writer based in Chicago. A self-taught guitarist and native Houstonian, Winter has been praised as a “generational talent” (NYCTaper) for his masterful instrumental music, which has received praise from Pitchfork, The Guardian, the Chicago Reader and others. He is a 2021 Luminarts Fellow in Creative Writing.
Check out his live session at Audiotree: https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=MdXMTxgi9Co

Ghost Bag
Ghost Bag is musician Nick Jongen. Formerly playing in bands like Allegorie, Sleep Kit and Baby Galaxy, Nick has moved into a more quieter, whispery voice for his solo project Ghost Bag, sometimes accompanied by his partner Tine Fetz. Armed with a sense of melody and experimentation Nick moves beyond quasi-singer-songwriter territory and opens up to a writing that incorporates soundscapes, noise and loops.
https://ghostbag.bandcamp.com/
