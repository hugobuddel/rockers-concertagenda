LIVE ON STAGE: GREEN POLICY

 

 

 

 

 

 

 

 

 

 

“From somewhere behind curtains of acid rain, a new name in the Dutch stoner rock scene has emerged. With gnarly riffs and messages of the post apocalyptic decay of the world, Green Policy turns every venue into a tornado. Led by frontman Dave Bouquet they thread silently through the haze of the Dutch underground until it’s time to rain down on the crowd. The rest of the band’s misfits consists out of the ever stoned Roan Grasmeijer on bass and the absolute madlad of a drummer, Milan Massar.”
