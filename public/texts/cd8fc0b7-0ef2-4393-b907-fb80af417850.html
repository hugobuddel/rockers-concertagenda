STEFAN CHRISTOFF (CAN) + ANDY MOOR (The EX) + LUKA SCHUURMAN
Sounding Worlds (piano / soundscape) | Intense Sound Signal Operates Without Warning (Solo Guitar, A/V set) | Snare Drum Static (for 9 drummers)

Stefan Christoff is a media maker, community activist and musician who lives in Montréal, Canada. Stefan hosts Free City Radio on CKUT and CJLO in Montreal, CKUW in Winnipeg, CFRC in Kingston, Ontario. Stefan coordinates Musicians for Palestine and makes music with many people (Records w/ Sam Shalabi, Lori Goldston and Anarchist Mountains). Stefan is on the board of the Immigrant Workers Centre and works with Cinema Politica Network. Stefan is also studying history at Concordia.

“Sounding Worlds” is the project, which he presented twice in Bulgaria last summer, this is the live set from last summer as recorded by the Bulgarian National Radio program Alarma Punk Jazz: https://soundcloud.com/freecityradio/stefan-christoff-sounding-worlds-national-gallery-concert-sofia-bulgaria-2022/s-u0nDlrTzOP1

Guitarist / Photographer Andy Moor. Moor’s own background is rooted in the post-punk Britain of the 1980s, but over the years he has collaborated with many musicians (Genevieve Murphy, DJ/rupture, Thurston Moore, Christine Abdelnour) from varied backgrounds and disciplines.

Snare Drum Static is Luka Schuurmans’ drone interpretation of a marching band; a collective snare drum roll stretched to extreme lengths, bringing hidden microrhythms and overtones to the surface.
