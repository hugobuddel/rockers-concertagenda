Al16 The Reunion will be the first official AL16 festival after our beloved home closed down. To recreate the original Archi vibe we set-up a program filled with homegrown art, bands and DJs. Several bands will be playing in the afternoon for the rock hard acoustic sounds, In the evening there will be a number of electronic DJs playing to create the night atmosphere.
